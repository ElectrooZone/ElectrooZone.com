Topics TYPES OF OPERATIONAL AMPLIFIER
Every Stage of an amplifier has 2 types of inputs:-

1. Inverting [ i.e, if input is +2V then the output will be +5V ].

2. Non-Inverting [ i.e, if the input is +2V then the output will be +5V].

---> There are basically varies types of amplifier as per their functions and these are as follows:-
  • Non-Inverting Amplifier.
  • Inverting Amplifier.
  • Unity Follower.
  • Differential Amplifier.
  • Instrumentation Amplifier.
