ACTIVE FILTERS AND LOW PASS FILTER

Active Filters :- A Filter is a circuit that passes a specified band of frequency & blocks (attenuate) all the frequencies signals outside the band.

Classification of Filters :

  1. Analog or Digital filters.
  2. Passive or Active filters.
  3. Audio or RF ( radio frequency) filters.
  • Analog filters are used to filter the analog signals while the digital filters use digital techniques for filtering of analog signals.

  • Passive filters are those filters which consists of passive components only such as resistance , capacitors & inductors.

  • Active filters are those filters which consists of passive as well as active devices (components) such as op-amp , transistors etc.

Frequency Response :

  • A Curve which shows the relation between the gain of the filter with frequency is called frequency response.

  • Mainly used Filters are as follows :-
    1. Low Pass Filters (LPF).

    2. High Pass Filters (HPF).

    3. Band Pass Filters (BPF).

    4. Band Reject Filters (BRF)--- [ Notch Filter].

    5. All Pass Filter (APF)---[ Phase Shift Filter].
